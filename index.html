Manufacturing Capabilities

The technology behind
every Kapzo product

Four proprietary processing platforms. One facility. The delivery science behind every Kapzo product — from white-label supplement formulations to sensory delivery systems and speciality food ingredients.

Capability 01
Precision Controlled-Release Delivery

The foundation of Kapzo's formulation differentiation. Individual active ingredients are coated with precision protective systems — at controlled thicknesses and precisely defined specifications — producing release profiles that are verified and reproduced batch-to-batch. Release can be triggered by pH, time, temperature, moisture, or mechanical action. This is what separates a Kapzo formulation from a commodity blend.

Release Type
Enteric (pH-Triggered)
Active ingredient bypasses the gastric environment and releases in the intestinal lumen. Applied to acid-sensitive enzymes and bile acids.
Release Type
Sustained / Extended
Active payload released over 3–6 hours. Applied to caffeine in PreMax Pro™ for a crash-free pre-workout experience.
Release Type
Moisture Barrier
Two incompatible actives kept isolated during storage, co-released at the target site. Applied in RespiCore Pro™ sulforaphane system.
Release Type
Sensory / Taste-Masked
Sulfur odor, mineral bitterness, and raw botanical off-notes eliminated. Applied to NAC, Ca-AKG, and berberine across the supplement range.
Capability 02
Powder Conversion & Stabilisation

Liquid extracts, botanical concentrates, oils, and emulsions converted into free-flowing, shelf-stable powders optimised for capsule, tablet, and RTM stick pack formats. Carrier systems are selected for the specific application — dispersibility, compression behaviour, moisture stability, and reconstitution speed. Used across the supplement, food ingredient, and sensory platforms. The JaggerPlus™ amorphous matrix stabilisation process is one output of this capability.

Application
Botanical Extracts
KSM-66 Ashwagandha, Rhodiola, Bacopa, Holy Basil and other standardised extracts converted to stable, free-flowing powder.
Application
Micronutrient Premix
Fat-soluble vitamins D3, K2, and CoQ10 micro-dispersed for uniform blend distribution and accurate fill weight.
Application
RTM Powder Production
Stick pack and sachet powders engineered for rapid cold-water dispersibility. Sensory and supplement platforms.
Application
Amorphous Stabilisation
Hygroscopic, crystallisation-prone ingredients — including jaggery and palm sugar — converted to shelf-stable amorphous powder form.
Capability 03
Granulation & Flowability Engineering

Multi-ingredient blends are processed to improve flow properties, reduce dust, eliminate segregation, and achieve uniform fill weights for high-speed capsule and sachet filling lines. Instantisation for RTM formats is a specific sub-capability — producing powders that fully disperse in cold water within seconds. Critical for consumer compliance in daily stick pack formats across the Kapzo Pro™ supplement range and the JaggerPlus™ and SatietyGLP Max™ RTM products.

Capability 04
Particle Size Control & Harmonisation

Where a single capsule contains multiple active populations — each with a different release profile — particle size harmonisation is the critical manufacturing step that determines fill weight consistency and dissolution reproducibility. Without it, different-sized populations separate during blending and filling, causing dose variation across batches. Applied to HepaGuard Pro™, RespiCore Pro™, and multi-active sensory products across the KapzoSphere™ range.
